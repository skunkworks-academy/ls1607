### Evidence status values

- **Planned** — expected but not started
- **In progress** — work exists but is incomplete
- **Submitted** — ready for review
- **Accepted** — reviewer confirms the stated outcome
- **Remediation required** — evidence does not yet meet the gate
- **Archived** — superseded while retained for history

## Evidence-quality test

Before evidence is marked accepted, confirm that it is:

- Relevant to a roadmap objective
- Produced by Luca or clearly identifies collaboration
- Reproducible or independently reviewable
- Sanitised of secrets and unnecessary personal data
- Stored in a durable location
- Dated and linked to a reviewer where required
- Accompanied by reflection or lessons learned
